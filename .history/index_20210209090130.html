The Parents and I

My brother and I were raised by my parents in Southern Louisiana. It was a great childhood. Who can ask for more than blow-up pools and mud parties? My parents gave us so much love growing up. We didn't know how lucky we were.


My parents must have been angels to have been able to put up with a child like me. I got into every kind of mess imaginable. Broken nose? Check! Stitches in lip? Check! Dog attack? Check! And every moment was fun! - for me, not my parents.


Despite all the trouble and trips to the Emergency Room, I don't remember them ever panicking or getting upset- not during those moments at least!


Nope, they only got mad when I cut my own hair, cut my brother's hair, wondered over to the neighbor's house, or didn't pick up my toys. The usual things

The best thing my parents have done for me was face my fears.
