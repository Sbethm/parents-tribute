Steven and Lori

Lori and Steven are two very simple names for two very ordinary and extraordinary people.

He was born in 1969. The same year that the first man stepped on the moon.

She was born in 1970. What happened this year? Oh, yeah! Mom was born!

Steven attended a small Catholic school from elementary school until high school graduation.

Lori attended a small public school throughout her elementary and high school education.

They married in ____ when Steven was 21 and Lori was 19 years old.

At the age of 21, Lori gave birth to Sarah (me!).

One year later (obviously not satisfied with their first child) Lori and Steven had their second child, a son named John.

My Parents and Me

Adventure at Splash Mountain

My parents are wonderful people, but like any child growing up, I would often wonder, "Why mom and dad? Why!" Whether it because of chores or going to on a scary ride at Disney World.

I had a lot of anxiety as a child (I still do!), and my parents knew this. Anxiety has been running rampant on both sides of my family, but despite this, I never its ugly face on my parents. Instead, they pushed me beyond my limits and made me face my fears.
