Before They Met

My parents were married at a young age. So, many of the stories they share are about their lives together.

They met, were married, and had children all at a young age, and they couldn't be happier with how their lives turned out. But, what happened before that?

Lori
Born in 1969, the same year that man first stepped on the moon.
He attended a small Catholic school.
During recess in the 4th grade, he gashed his elbow while playing a fierce game of football. This resulted in four stitches which he is still very proud of today.
When he was thirteen, he camped out in his backyard and stayed up all night, feeling like a young adventurer.
He ran his first high school track meet, the 100 yard dash, and won third place. For his first track meet, that's not too shabby!
He was in a car accident, of course, it was "not his fault", which lead to more stitches but this time in his forehead. Because of this, he had to sit out his senior year homecoming football game. While he may have missed the game, he probably got plenty of attention for the ladies.
He took the leading role of Jesus during his high school senior year which from then on became an annual event. No doubt each year ever Jesus was unable to capture the magic of his first performance.
He had a dream of marrying a beautiful wife and having children to enjoy playing games with. This dream came to bite him in the butt because his kids kick his ass at those games now!

In a second attempt to try the daredevil's life, she rode a in the Appalachians. This second and last attempt led her to conclude that such daring feats were not for her.
